About me

I am a fifth-year PhD student in the Institute of Operations Research and Analytics at National University of Singapore. I am jointly advised by Chung Piaw Teo and Xiaobo Li. My current research focuses on stochastic optimization, robust optimization, and online learning with applications to pricing and revenue management problems. I am also interested in operational problems in energy and sustainability. Prior to graduate study, I worked as a Design Engineer in construction industry for three years.

I am on the 2021-2022 academic job market.

Research Interests

My research interests include

  • Stochastic and Robust Optimization

  • Data-driven Decision Making

  • Online Learning and Online Convex Optimization

  • Revenue Management and Pricing Analytics

  • Discrete Choice Modeling
